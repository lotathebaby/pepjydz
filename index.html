OUR SERVICES

Ship Repairs

We engage in ship repairs and refits, major conversion work, steel repairs and construction, vessel upgrades, ship life extension programmes, hull and tank blasting, preparation and painting.

Marine Operational Support

We engage in the on-site heavy-lift transportation of offshore assets, management of projects, coordination, supervision and commissioning services.

Maritime Logistics

We deal with the transportation, importation and charter of vessels, vessel spares, stores and consumables. We ensure the flow of materials from suppliers into an organization.

Marine Consultancy

We are concerned with the inspection and survey, crew welfare and various manning solutions, acquisition of marine equipment and sales and regulatory activities such as registrations and licensing of the organization.

Marine Engineering

We deal with rig support, piping work, valve, pump repairs and services, non-destructive tests, radar servicing and repairs, afloat ship repairs, steering gear repairs, supply and structural maintenance of well head, etc.

New Building Project

Some of the expertise we offer include but are not limited to equipment procurement, newbuilding project supervision, contract negotiation and technical coordination.
